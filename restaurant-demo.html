Our Story

A Passion for
Authentic Taste

Savanna Kitchen was born from a dream — to bring the warmth of East African home cooking to a world-class dining experience. Every dish tells a story of our land, our people, and our heritage.


We source all ingredients from local Ugandan farmers, supporting our community while delivering the freshest flavours to your plate.
